Tired of being sidelined? So is the Princess. Sick of outsourcing heroics, this royal isn't waiting for anyone to save her this time. The Princess is suiting up to dismantle the legacy of GhostFace, son of GhostFace, son of — wait, who was it again? Oh right, Ghosty (her old frenemy, apparently). Brace yourselves, the high heels are on and they're made for stomping!

Welcome to 'The Curse of the Castle Creep,' the uproarious installment where our Princess becomes her own hero, the queen of her own pixelated destiny. Forget sending minions; she's soloing this dungeon crawl quest to reclaim her crown, her gold, and her Wi-Fi password from her haunted castle.

You think you can stop her? Ha! You ARE her. So hit start and unleash royal mayhem. The throne has waited long enough. It's time for the game where princesses don't wait for rescue — they roll up their sleeves and get the job done!
